RobotKI

Robot Knowledge Inventory

Dates: Jul 2021 - Jun 2023

Role: Lead Developer (PDRA)

Overview

RobotKI (Robot Knowledge Inventory) was an open-source repository designed to centralise robotics resources - including tutorials, digital assets, simulation environments, software packages, and other technical materials - to support developers, researchers, and students working in robotics.

Contribution

As part of my postdoctoral research, I served as the Lead Developer of the online platform. My work spanned data mining, backend and frontend development, producing technical documentation, and actively engaging with stakeholders to shape the platform’s functionality and scope. RobotKI later became the precursor to a proprietary IP within the CRADLE Prosperity Partnership, evolving into foundational tooling for a broader robot toolchain now being applied across multiple high-value domains.

Due to the project’s commercial nature, access to RobotKI is currently under embargo.
