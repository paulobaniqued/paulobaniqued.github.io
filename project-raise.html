Robotic Automation of Innovative Spark Erosion with Scintam Engineering

Dates: Apr 2024 - Oct 2024

Role: Technical Project Manager

Overview

The RAISE (Robotic Automation of Innovative Spark Erosion) project was born from the CRADLE IdEAs (Industry Engagement Activity) process. This was done as a collaboration with startup SME Scintam Engineering who have developed unique portable EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) equipment â€“ designed for the fast removal of seized fasteners such as bolts or rivets, that may otherwise result in long delays at overhaul. This is a new technology with high value potential to several industries, in particular as part of a roboticised solution to allow for remote operation and automation in demanding and long-lasting environments. CRADLE led a three-month technical development programme culminating in a live technology demonstration to industry professionals, hosted at the Amentum laboratories in Birchwood. The showcased technology comprises a base X2 tracked robot platform for navigation, with a UR10 manipulator arm to allow for dextrous control of the EDM probe, which is integrated as a payload.
