Voolsi

Neurotech Startup for Mental Health Monitoring

Dates: Jun-20

Role: Co-Founder and Product Engineer

Overview

Voolsi is a neurotech startup that helps people monitor their physical and mental health through an earbud device. The project was a participant in The Global Hack (2020).

Contribution

I supported the team in building the product and contributed during the initial stages of ideation and concept development. Additionally, I provided remote product design and prototyping support during the COVID-19 lockdown.
